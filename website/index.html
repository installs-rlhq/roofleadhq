Frequently Asked Questions

What does customized, founder-led setup include?+

Setup is customized and founder-led. We configure RoofLeadHQ around your lead sources, service area, qualification questions, follow-up preferences, and calendar workflow.

After setup is complete and you go live, RoofLeadHQ answers calls, captures form leads, follows up automatically, and helps get qualified homeowners onto your calendar.

Every plan gets the same setup and workflow — the only difference is monthly lead volume. $199 setup. Cancel anytime. No contracts.

How much does RoofLeadHQ AI handle?+

RoofLeadHQ AI handles the repetitive parts of lead response and follow-up: fast first replies, qualification questions, automated follow-ups, confirmations, reminders, and missed-lead recovery using your configured preferences. It books homeowner inspections on the roofer’s calendar.

You stay in control of sales decisions and exceptions. When judgment or a personal conversation is needed, the lead is surfaced clearly so you or your team can step in.

What happens if a lead needs a human touch?+

If a homeowner asks something outside the normal workflow, has a complex issue, or needs direct attention, the lead is surfaced so you or your team can step in. RoofLeadHQ AI does not replace good customer service — it makes sure fewer leads are missed or delayed.

You receive the homeowner’s contact information and project details so you can call, text, or follow up directly when needed.

What happens to the lead sources I already use?+

You keep using your current lead sources. RoofLeadHQ works with Google Business Profile, website forms, Facebook, ads, calls, texts, email, and other channels homeowners already use to reach you.

For calls and texts, RoofLeadHQ provides a dedicated number option. Responses can go out through the same channels the homeowner used so no lead goes unanswered.

RoofLeadHQ does not replace your marketing. It improves what happens after a homeowner reaches out.

Can RoofLeadHQ keep up during busy seasons or storm season?+

Yes. Busy seasons and storms are a primary reason RoofLeadHQ exists.

When volume spikes, RoofLeadHQ AI keeps fast response and automated follow-up going so new homeowners still hear back quickly instead of waiting hours or days. It surfaces urgent and storm leads clearly while you stay focused on active work and crews.

It gives you more capacity for lead-to-inspection conversion without adding more admin work to your day. Closing the gap between roofing lead and booked inspection.

What if an insurance claim is involved?+

RoofLeadHQ AI can ask insurance-related qualification questions and capture important details before the site visit — whether a claim has been filed, whether the homeowner has spoken with their insurer, and what damage is being reported.

Those details can be included so you know what you are walking into before the inspection.

Do I lose control of the conversation?+

No. You are still in control of sales decisions and any conversation that needs personal judgment. RoofLeadHQ AI handles the repetitive response and follow-up. When a lead needs your direct attention, it is surfaced clearly with full context.

You receive the homeowner’s contact information and project details for the relationship and the sale.

Which states do you serve?+

RoofLeadHQ can serve roofing companies throughout the United States.

Our standard service area includes the 48 contiguous states. If you are located in Alaska or Hawaii, reach out and we can confirm whether RoofLeadHQ is a good fit for your market and setup needs.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. No contracts. Cancel whenever you want.

What if I go over my lead cap?+

We’ll work with you on a solution — usually a prorated upgrade or a custom plan. Our signup form states: “I understand that pricing is based on the standard lead volume included with my selected tier. If my monthly lead volume exceeds this amount, RoofLeadHQ will adjust pricing in the next billing cycle and will provide at least 5 days’ notice via email.”

Is there a free trial?+

No free trial, but you can cancel anytime. We use intro pricing to make it easy to get started — $199/mo for the first 3 months on Starter.

Do you integrate with my CRM?+

CSV export and webhook destinations are on the near-term roadmap. Native JobNimbus, AccuLynx, and ServiceTitan integrations are not currently built.

How do Angi/Thumbtack leads work?+

Email-forward intake is on the near-term roadmap. Official Angi/Thumbtack/Yelp API integrations are not currently built. Today, when one of these leads comes in, you can send the homeowner’s number to your RoofLeadHQ number and we handle fast response and follow-up from there.
