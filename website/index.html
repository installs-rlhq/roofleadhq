Frequently Asked Questions

What does the Founder-Led Launch Program include?+

During the Founder-Led Launch Program, we set up your dedicated number, intake, qualification support, review queue, dashboard, and coordination for booking inspections and appointments.

We monitor launch activity under founder/operator review to make sure leads are being captured, coordinated, and tracked correctly.

During launch, day-to-day activity stays under review and coordination. Live workflow activation only after explicit approval and readiness review. Nothing is system-led until approved.

How much does RoofLeadHQ handle for us?+

During the Founder-Led Launch Program, RoofLeadHQ supports the repetitive parts of lead intake and qualification with drafts and tracking prepared for founder/operator review: reply support, qualification questions, follow-up coordination, confirmations, reminders, and reporting.

You and the founder/operator stay in control. Coordination during launch routes anything needing direct attention to the review queue.

What happens if a lead needs a human touch?+

If a homeowner asks something outside the normal workflow, has a complex issue, or needs direct attention, the lead is flagged in the review queue so the founder/operator or you can step in.

The goal is not to replace good customer service. The goal is to make sure fewer leads are missed, delayed, or forgotten through structured review and coordination during launch.

What happens to the lead sources I already use?+

You can keep using your current lead sources. RoofLeadHQ works with the places homeowners already contact you, including your website, Google Business Profile, Facebook, ads, mailers, email, and other lead channels.

For calls and texts, RoofLeadHQ provides a dedicated number that can be used on your lead sources. For homeowners who submit a form or send an email, RoofLeadHQ can respond through those same channels so no lead goes unanswered regardless of how they reach out.

RoofLeadHQ does not replace your marketing. It helps improve what happens after a homeowner reaches out.

Can RoofLeadHQ keep up during busy seasons or storm season?+

Yes. Busy seasons are one of the main reasons RoofLeadHQ exists.

When call and message volume spikes, the Founder-Led Launch Program helps make sure new homeowners still receive a faster first response instead of waiting hours or days. It supports collecting project details, identifying urgent opportunities, and coordination to move qualified leads toward inspections while you stay focused on active work.

It gives you more capacity without adding more admin work to your day, with all steps under review during launch.

What if an insurance claim is involved?+

RoofLeadHQ can ask insurance-related qualification questions and capture important details before the site visit, such as whether a claim has already been filed, whether the homeowner has spoken with their insurance company, and what kind of damage they are reporting.

Those details can be included in your lead notification and calendar invite so you know what you are walking into before the appointment.

What if a lead needs a human touch?+

You are still in control. During launch, initial response support, communication drafts, qualification, and scheduling coordination are prepared under founder/operator review. You or the founder can step in at the review queue whenever a lead needs direct personal conversation.

When a lead is reviewed or an inspection is coordinated, you receive the homeowner’s contact information and project details so you can call, text, or follow up directly when needed.

RoofLeadHQ does not replace the roofer during the Founder-Led Launch Program. It provides coordination support and review queue so you can focus on the relationship and the sale.

Which states do you serve?+

RoofLeadHQ can serve roofing companies throughout the United States.

Our standard service area includes the 48 contiguous states. If you are located in Alaska or Hawaii, reach out and we can confirm whether RoofLeadHQ is a good fit for your market and setup needs.
