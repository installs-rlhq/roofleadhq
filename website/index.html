Frequently Asked Questions

What does setup include?+

We set it up with you in your first week. On the call we configure your lead sources, service area, qualifying questions, follow-up, and calendar workflow — no tech skills needed on your end. Every plan gets the same setup; the only difference is monthly lead volume.

How much does the AI handle, and do I stay in control?+

The AI handles the repetitive parts: fast first replies, qualifying questions, follow-ups, confirmations, reminders, and booking inspections on your calendar. You stay in control of every sales decision.

When a lead needs judgment or a personal conversation, it's surfaced with full context and you get the homeowner's contact info and project details so you can step in directly.

Do I need to change how I get leads?+

No. It works with the sources you already use — Google Business Profile, website forms, Facebook, ads, calls, texts, and email — and provides a dedicated number for calls and texts. It doesn't replace your marketing; it improves what happens after a homeowner reaches out.

Got a lead from Angi, Thumbtack, or a referral? Send the homeowner's number to your RoofLeadHQ number and it takes the follow-up from there. (Automatic email-forward intake for those platforms is on the near-term roadmap.)

Can it keep up during storm season?+

Yes — that's a big reason it exists. When volume spikes, every homeowner still hears back in seconds instead of waiting hours or days, and urgent storm leads are surfaced clearly while you stay focused on active work and crews.

What if an insurance claim is involved?+

It asks insurance-related questions up front — whether a claim's been filed, whether the homeowner has spoken with their insurer, and what damage is reported — and attaches those details so you know what you're walking into before the inspection.

Which states do you serve?+

Roofing companies throughout the United States. Our standard service area is the 48 contiguous states. In Alaska or Hawaii? Reach out and we'll confirm whether it's a good fit for your market.

How do pricing and cancellation work?+

One-time $199 setup, then intro pricing for your first 3 months before your plan's standard rate. No contracts, cancel anytime, no free trial.

Go over your lead cap and we'll work it out — usually a prorated upgrade or a custom plan. Our signup form states: "I understand that pricing is based on the standard lead volume included with my selected tier. If my monthly lead volume exceeds this amount, RoofLeadHQ will adjust pricing in the next billing cycle and will provide at least 5 days' notice via email."

Do you integrate with my CRM?+

CSV export and webhook destinations are on the near-term roadmap. Native JobNimbus, AccuLynx, and ServiceTitan integrations are not currently built.
