# First Paid Contractor Kickoff Email Draft

Date: 2026-06-05

## Purpose

Use this draft for the first contractor-facing kickoff email in the RoofLeadHQ Founder-Led Launch Program.

It sets expectations for the first launch phase, points the contractor to the launch packet and intake worksheet, and avoids promises about guaranteed outcomes or automated volume.

Reference docs:

- Launch packet: `docs/FIRST_PAID_PILOT_LAUNCH_PACKET.md`
- Contractor intake worksheet: `docs/FIRST_PAID_CONTRACTOR_INTAKE_WORKSHEET.md`

## Subject Line Options

- RoofLeadHQ kickoff for your Founder-Led Launch Program
- Your RoofLeadHQ kickoff and dashboard access
- Next steps for your RoofLeadHQ launch

## Short Kickoff Email Draft

Hi [Contractor Name],

Thanks again for moving forward with RoofLeadHQ.

You are in the Founder-Led Launch Program, which means I’ll help set up the workflow, monitor the early activity, and keep things organized while we review leads and work toward helping you book inspections / book appointments.

I’ll send over dashboard access and the launch packet so you can see the process in one place. Please review the intake worksheet as well so I have the business details, service area, booking process, reporting preferences, and homeowner contact rules I need from you.

What to expect:

- A read-only dashboard for visibility into the launch
- Founder-led setup and monitoring during the first phase
- Manual review of lead flow and contact rules before anything is expanded
- No automated production outreach unless it is explicitly approved later

What I need from you:

- Business details and owner/contact info
- Service area and preferred lead sources
- Your booking process and dashboard recipient
- Manual Outreach setup notes, Vapi phone lead intake notes, reporting preferences, and homeowner contact rules

I’ll keep the launch process simple and clear. If you send me the requested info, I’ll take the next setup steps and keep you posted.

Best,
Jason

## What The Contractor Should Expect

- Founder-led setup and monitoring
- Dashboard access for the launch view
- A controlled launch process that focuses on review and coordination
- Help booking inspections / booking appointments where appropriate
- No promise of jobs, revenue, or fixed appointment volume
- No automated production outreach without explicit approval

## Notes For Jason

- Keep the tone direct and practical.
- Reference the launch packet and intake worksheet rather than repeating setup details in email.
- Avoid promising automated lead volume, booked inspections or booked appointments, or revenue outcomes.
- Keep the email aligned with the Founder-Led Launch Program framing.
