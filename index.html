My Story

I was talking to a friend in the roofing business one day, and he told me how many leads they miss because good, affordable systems to help book leads are hard to come by. He’d be up on the roof when a homeowner called, or miss multiple calls at 2 AM during storm season. By the time he checked his phone, they’d already booked with other roofers. He lost count of how many jobs he missed.

Hearing his story made me realize there had to be a better way to help roofers like him. That’s when I built the first version of RoofLeadHQ AI — not as a generic chatbot, but as a custom system built specifically for roofing contractors. Now the RoofLeadHQ AI handles every lead 24/7, qualifies them, and books appointments directly on their calendar. They don't have to miss a qualified lead anymore.

I’ve built RoofLeadHQ to help roofing contractors book more appointments, handle less admin work, and gain peace of mind knowing that no lead will ever go cold again.
