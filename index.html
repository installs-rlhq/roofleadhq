Frequently Asked Questions

How is RoofLeadHQ different from other roofing lead sites?+

RoofLeadHQ is not a lead-selling service. Other lead platforms can send you leads, but you still have to chase them, qualify them, and try to book the appointment yourself.

RoofLeadHQ works with the leads you already generate from your website, Google Business Profile, Facebook, ads, referrals, and other sources. We help respond, communicate, qualify, and schedule those homeowners so more of your existing leads turn into booked appointments.

You do not have to stop using other lead sources. RoofLeadHQ helps you get more value from the ones you already have.

What does the 7-day pilot include?+

During the 7-day pilot, we custom-build your RoofLeadHQ system for your roofing business, connect it to your lead sources, and run it live so you can see how it works with real homeowners.

The custom build includes your dedicated lead response number, qualification flow, homeowner messaging, appointment scheduling process, and roofer notifications.

The pilot has no monthly charge. If RoofLeadHQ does not book at least 5 qualified appointments during the 7-day pilot window, your first month is free. The $499 setup fee covers the custom build and launch of your system.

How long does setup take, and what is involved?+

Most roofing companies are up and running in about one business day, depending on how many lead sources and scheduling details need to be connected.

We handle the setup for you. That includes building your response flow, creating your dedicated lead number, setting up qualification questions, connecting your calendar process, and making sure you receive lead and appointment notifications.

No tech skills are needed on your end.

What happens to the lead sources I already use?+

You can keep using your current lead sources. RoofLeadHQ works with the places homeowners already contact you, including your website, Google Business Profile, Facebook, ads, mailers, email, and other lead channels.

For calls and texts, RoofLeadHQ provides a dedicated number that can be used on your lead sources. For homeowners who submit a form or send an email, RoofLeadHQ can respond through those same channels so no lead goes unanswered regardless of how they reach out.

RoofLeadHQ does not replace your marketing. It helps improve what happens after a homeowner reaches out.

Can RoofLeadHQ keep up during busy seasons or storm season?+

Yes. Busy seasons are one of the main reasons RoofLeadHQ exists.

When call and message volume spikes, RoofLeadHQ helps make sure new homeowners still receive a fast response instead of waiting hours or days for a callback. It can collect job details, identify urgent opportunities, and help move qualified leads toward your calendar while you stay focused on active jobs.

It gives you more capacity without adding more admin work to your day.

What if an insurance claim is involved?+

RoofLeadHQ can ask insurance-related qualification questions and capture important details before the site visit, such as whether a claim has already been filed, whether the homeowner has spoken with their insurance company, and what kind of damage they are reporting.

Those details can be included in your lead notification and calendar invite so you know what you are walking into before the appointment.

What if a lead needs a human touch?+

You are still in control. RoofLeadHQ handles the initial response, communication, qualification, and scheduling, but you can step in whenever a lead needs a personal conversation.

When a lead comes in or books an appointment, you receive the homeowner’s contact information and job details so you can call, text, or follow up directly when needed.

RoofLeadHQ does not replace the roofer. It clears the admin work so you can focus on the relationship and the sale.

Which states do you serve?+

RoofLeadHQ can serve roofing companies throughout the United States.

Our standard service area includes the 48 contiguous states. If you are located in Alaska or Hawaii, reach out and we can confirm whether RoofLeadHQ is a good fit for your market and setup needs.
