Frequently Asked Questions

How long does setup take?+

Most roofing companies are up and running in less than a day. We handle the custom build and setup for you — no tech skills needed.

Do I need to change how I get leads?+

We give you a dedicated number to use for your lead sources — Google Business Profile, website, Facebook, ads, and more. That’s the main change. From there, RoofLeadHQ handles the response and booking process.

What if a lead needs a human touch?+

You’re notified when leads come in and when appointments are booked. RoofLeadHQ handles the admin side so you can step in when the relationship or sales conversation needs you.

Does it work during storm season?+

Yes. That’s one of the main reasons RoofLeadHQ exists. When lead volume spikes, every lead still gets an immediate response and urgent opportunities can be flagged.

Does it handle insurance claims?+

Yes. RoofLeadHQ can ask insurance-related qualification questions and capture important details before the site visit.

What states do you serve?+

All 48 contiguous states.

How is this different from Angi or Thumbtack?+

Angi and Thumbtack sell leads you still have to chase. RoofLeadHQ works on the leads you already get and helps convert them into booked appointments.

What does the 7-day pilot look like?+

We custom-build your RoofLeadHQ system and run it for 7 days with no monthly charge. If it does not book at least 5 extra qualified appointments during that pilot window, your first month is free. The $499 setup fee covers the custom build.
